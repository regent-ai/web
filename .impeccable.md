## Design Context

### Users
This app is for Regent operators, token holders, agent builders, and technically fluent community members. They use it to understand the ecosystem, connect wallets, redeem assets, inspect token structure, view public operator reports, and move between Regent web products and CLI flows without losing context.

### Brand Personality
Brand tone is sovereign, technical, and ceremonial. It should feel confident and onchain-native without becoming cold, cramped, or jargon-heavy. The interface should read as deliberate infrastructure for serious operators, not as a generic SaaS dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use the existing cream, ink, gold, and charcoal palette as the default visual language, with Regent scene work as a supporting accent rather than the only source of meaning. Prefer light surfaces, tactile borders, strong pixel typography, and spacious compositions that stay usable on small screens. Avoid interchangeable startup UI patterns, purple neon palettes, and decorative motion that hides content or blocks action.

### Design Principles
1. Keep the product fully usable on a phone without hiding critical routes or actions.
2. Let Regent scenes add character, but never make them the only way a person understands a page.
3. Turn dense operational data into readable stacks, labeled cards, or scrollable rails on narrow screens.
4. Use plain-English copy and obvious action labels before cleverness.
5. Preserve the brand’s structured, ceremonial feel while keeping tap targets large and layouts calm.
